ABOUT DEEPHUNT
--------------
DeepHunt is an all-in-one cybersecurity training platform built for aspiring
analysts, SOC operators, and incident responders. It combines a structured
learning academy, real-world cyber range labs, and an AI-powered scoring engine
into a single, seamless experience. Whether you're a beginner learning to
triage alerts or a seasoned professional sharpening your threat-hunting skills,
DeepHunt adapts to your level and pushes you further.


PLATFORM PILLARS
----------------

1. ACADEMY — Learn the Fundamentals & Beyond
   DeepHunt Academy offers curated cybersecurity courses spanning SOC operations,
   incident response, digital forensics, network analysis, and threat hunting.
   Each course features video lessons with chapter navigation, embedded quizzes,
   downloadable resources (cheatsheets, PDFs), and auto-saved progress tracking.
   On completion, learners earn verifiable certificates and skill badges.

2. LABS — Practice in a Live Cyber Range
   The Labs environment is where theory meets practice. Users step into realistic
   SOC Simulator and Incident Response Simulator scenarios powered by live data.
   The SOC Simulator presents a SIEM-style interface with real-time alerts that
   users must triage — acknowledge, escalate, dismiss, or investigate — just like
   a production security operations centre. The IR Simulator guides users through
   playbook-driven investigations, forensics challenges, log analysis, and
   artefact examination, culminating in a graded incident report submission.

3. HONEYPOT ENGINE — Real Attacks, Safe Environment
   At the heart of DeepHunt's realism is a built-in honeypot engine. Powered by
   Cowrie (SSH) and Dionaea (HTTP, SMB, FTP), the platform deploys intentionally
   vulnerable services inside a fully isolated Docker network. These honeypots
   attract and capture real-world attack patterns — brute-force attempts, malware
   drops, lateral movement — and stream all events into the labs in real time.
   Full packet capture is available per session, giving learners hands-on
   experience with genuine adversary behaviour in a completely safe sandbox.

4. AI-POWERED DUAL SCORING ENGINE
   DeepHunt doesn't just check if your answer is right — it evaluates how you
   work. The Response Score (R_score) uses NLP and semantic similarity to grade
   quiz answers, incident reports, and flag captures against reference solutions.
   The Behaviour Score (B_score) analyses your working patterns — command
   sequences, navigation efficiency, hesitation timing, and confidence — using
   machine-learning models. Both scores are fused into a single Final Score with
   integrity checks, giving learners an honest, cheat-resistant assessment of
   their true skill level.


KEY FEATURES

• Adaptive Difficulty      — The platform increases scenario complexity as your
                             skills improve.
• Real-Time Score Widget   — See your live performance score update as you work
                             through lab sessions.
• Skill Radar Chart        — Visualise your strengths across SOC, IR, Forensics,
                             Threat Hunting, and Network Analysis.
• Progress Tracking        — Monitor courses in progress, completed labs, streaks,
                             and historical score trends.
• Auto-Generated Certs     — Receive PDF certificates upon course completion.
• Role-Based Access        — Separate dashboards for learners and administrators.
• SIEM & Monitoring        — Admins access Kibana dashboards for log analysis and
                             Grafana for infrastructure metrics.
• Fully Containerised      — Every service runs via Docker Compose behind a
                             single Nginx reverse proxy — deploy anywhere.
