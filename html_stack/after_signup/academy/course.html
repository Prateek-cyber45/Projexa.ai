About this lesson

In this introductory lesson, you'll learn what a Security Operations Center (SOC) is, its pivotal role in an organization's security posture, and the typical day-to-day workflow of SOC analysts (Tier 1 to Tier 3). We will cover the foundational theory of threat detection, response playbooks, and how alerts traverse through a SIEM.

Key Learning Objectives:

  • Understand the Triad of People, Process, and Technology in a SOC.
  • Differentiate between Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 analysts.
  • Explain the alert fatigue phenomenon and basic triage strategies.
  • Identify the core components of a SIEM (Security Information & Event Management).
